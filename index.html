About Me

Hi there! My name is Brandon Phillips, I'm 20 years old, and I'm currently in the process of receiving my full stack web developer certification through the University of Washington. I attended Montana State University for one year, but I wasn't able to find the career path that fit me. I then took some time away from school to try out different career paths, though it took some time until I found what I think is right for me. After a year of failed attempts at finding my career path, the lockdown began. What started out as me just learning a new skill to keep my mind busy quickly turned into what I believe to be the career path that checks all the boxes for me. I hope to use this certification in web development as a way to 'break in' to the coding industry so that I can later on work into a more software development oriented career, working with languages such as python and java.
